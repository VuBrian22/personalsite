Things to do before I service your machine

  • Backup
  • Secure

It's always a good idea to backup all your photos, important documents, music, and other files you might want to keep for the future. You should keep these backup files somewhere safe; maybe on a USB Drive or in the cloud.

Securing your accounts might seem a bit off, however if your computer has been malware/spywar/virus ridden, you might just want to check up on all your accounts. Just in case there is a compromise of your personal data.
